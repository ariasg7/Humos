Our Story

At HUMOS NY, we are passionate about fire and lovers of great food. We specialize in grills, sandwiches, and catering services for events in the vibrant city of New York.

With a fusion of Latin-American flavors, we offer a unique gastronomic experience that will delight your senses.

Our focus is on providing you with quality service, using the finest ingredients, and delivering personalized attention at every event.

Whether you’re looking to delight your guests with a delicious grill feast, organize a corporate event, or simply enjoy the best sandwiches in town, HUMOS NY is ready to exceed your expectations.

Sandwiches

Indulge in the best sandwiches in NY. Our sandwiches are crafted with the finest ingredients, grilled to perfection, and served with homemade sauces. Each bite is a burst of flavors that will satisfy your cravings.

Catering

Elevate your event with our professional catering services. Whether it’s a corporate gathering, a celebration, or a food festival, our team will work closely with you to create a customized menu that suits your preferences and dietary requirements.
